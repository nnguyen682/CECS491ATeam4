Good Vibes Summarized

Good Vibes is an Amazon Alexa skill that aims to assist users with depression.
Although Alexa may not be physically present, her interactions will be conversational
in the form of a voice user interface.

Why did we create Good Vibes?

According to TechCrunch, 16 percent of Americans own a smartspeaker.(1) More specifically,
GeekWire states that there are more than 8 million people that own an Alexa Device.(2) Healthline
stated in an article that there is a recorded amount of 16 million cases of people that experienced
at least one major depressive episode in 2012.(3)

Depression takes on many forms and is often difficult to diagnose. It can be common that somebody
in a current state of depression does not have the desire to seek help, let alone leave their house.
This, is where our application has the benefit of easy in-home access for anybody with an Echo device.

This web page will show you how to use the Good Vibes Alexa skill.

Main Features

Assessment Tests

The assessment test is based off of the Patient Health Questionaire (PHQ-9) which will determine the severity
of depression. Test scores are saved for future reference to validate any progress in helping with
depression. Tests can be taken an unlimited amount of times, where the most recent test score will
overwrite any previous test scores on the account.

Discussions

Users will be able to talk about how they are feeling to Good Vibes and the skill will try to provide useful
responses. If the user states that they are having a nice day, then Good Vibes may respond with positive reinforcement
such as “Great moves, keep it up, proud of you”. If the user states that they are feeling upset, then Good Vibes might
give the user a suggestion such as “ You deserve some relaxation. How about watching a movie to take your mind off of things?”
or Good Vibes might try to give other forms of support like, “I am here for you and I am not going anywhere.”

Stories

Nobody is alone when it comes to depression. The developers of Good Vibes felt that this message is important for those
that may be experiencing a warped perception of the world that provokes that mentality. To combat this mindset
Good Vibes has personal stories from the developers that expand on not only the hardships of depression, but ways
to overcome it and grow from it.

Advice

Understanding depression is a huge part of treating it. Good Vibes has a collection of advice that relates
to tips for coping with depression and ways to spot symptoms of depression.

Trigger Words

Depression cannot be taken lightly, we have to consider extreme cases. This might include suicidal or self-harmful thoughts, that
Good Vibes will try to hear out. If an event like this were to occur, Good Vibes will suggest seeking out help while
also reassuring that the user still has a purpose in life. Good Vibes will also provide the number for the suicide hotline.

Getting Started

1 - Use the invocation name “Good Vibes” to start up the skill.
2 - Select an option from the following choices: assessment test, state of mind, or help.
3 - Saying the word help will prompt Alexa to speak out the available options within the skill.
4 - If you select the assessment test option, Alexa will read out the test questions while you provide an honest answer. At the end of the test, Alexa will tell you the level of severity of the depression you are experiencing
5 - Selecting the option to discuss your state of mind will prompt Alexa to ask you how you are feeling. After responding to Alexa, she will provide a suggestion or give a helpful response.
6 - Using the phrase “Alexa stop” or not giving a response for an extended period of time will end the Good Vibes session.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Will my data be kept private?
     A: Yes! Your data will be kept private and will not be released to the public.

Q: How do I start using the Good Vibes skill?
     A: Once you have enabled in the skill by following the instructions above, simply tell Alexa to “open Good Vibes.”

Q: How will Good Vibes actually help me cope with my depression?
     A: Good Vibes enables Alexa to give meaningful responses to the clinically depressed. After repeated use, Alexa will be able to give suggestions based on previous activity.

Q: Why should I choose Good Vibes instead of using traditional measures to aid depression?
     A: Good Vibes provides a human-like interaction that is both free and convenient for users. Alexa is a great alternative for those that do not want to talk to anyone about their depression. Users will not have to live with the fear of judgment using Good Vibes since Alexa is here with the sole purpose of providing assistance.

Q: What does the assessment test determine?
     A: There are two types of assessment tests. One of the tests will diagnose the severity of your depression and it is based on the Patient Health Questionaire (PHQ-9) document. The other assessment test will be used to determine the most likely type of depression you may be experiencing and it is based on an article from the Better Help website.
